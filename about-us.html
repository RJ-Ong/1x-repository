Our Story

A Taste of Home, A Journey of Heart

Before JPO Fishballs, Burgers, and Shawarma became a part of the community, it all started with a simple dream — and an unforgettable flavor.

Long ago, our founder’s grandmother spent years working far from home, in a bustling city filled with new sights, sounds, and tastes. Among those experiences, there was one flavor that stood out — a sauce so distinct that it left a lasting impression on her heart. Every day, she'd make trips just to bring that special taste back to her workplace, carrying with her a little piece of happiness in every cup.

But life has a way of inspiring innovation. With a little curiosity and a lot of determination, she and a close friend began experimenting in their small kitchen. After countless tries, laughter, and even a few mishaps, they crafted a sauce that captured the spirit of what they loved most — without needing to search far for it.

When she returned home, this treasured flavor didn’t just stay a family secret; it became a symbol of perseverance and love. Over time, with careful adjustments and a heart full of dreams, the sauce evolved — ready to serve not just family, but the entire community.

Thus, JPO was born: a small venture built on big dreams, with a recipe perfected through generations, and a promise that every dish would carry the same warmth, dedication, and tradition that started it all.

From a distant kitchen across the sea to your plate today — a journey of flavors, a story of family
